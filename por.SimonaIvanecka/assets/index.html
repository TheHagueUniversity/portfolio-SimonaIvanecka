My work

Please have a look at some of my previous work!

Travel App

I create this app for the final assignment of the course Building Engaging Prototypes. I had to work with given logo and colorpalette. My task was to make this travel app easy to use while include all the necessary features.

Glasses

I worked on this project, during my exchange program in The King Mongut University in Thailand. Final assignment for class jewellery and small metallurgy was to create jewellery while using bending technique and concept of the work was supposed to be connected with my major.

The Dutch Store

My task was to recreate old “The Dutch Store” website. Before diving in I had to make an expert review and target group persona on the existing website. Based on my findings, I recreated the design, added new functions and made the website more user friendly.

Xdemia

I worked on this design challenge, starting with the research about what students find "sexy" about the interface, especially when is connected with educational platform. after collecting all my findings, I created and presented my final idea/design.
